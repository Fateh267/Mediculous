Anovate Cream is a combination medicine used for the treatment of piles (hemorrhoids). It relieves the pain, swelling, itching and discomfort associated with passing of stools in people who suffer this problem in the anal area.Anovate Cream must be used as per doctor's prescription. It is for external use only. Before using the medicine check the label for directions of use and apply gently on the clean and dry affected area. You should always wash your hands after applying it. Avoid getting it into your eyes or mouth. If accidental exposure occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.There are no major side effects associated with this medicine but can cause application site burning or irritation. Do not use this medicine if you are allergic to it.
