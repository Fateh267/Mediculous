Airtec FB 400 Instacap is a combination of two medicines in one inhaler. It relieves the long-term symptoms of asthma and COPD, making breathing easier. It works by inhibiting the release of certain chemical messengers that cause inflammation (swelling) and relaxes the muscles in the airways.This medicine is for inhalation purpose only. Thus, it should not be taken by mouth. It is important to follow all the guidelines of doctor before using it. However, before using it, inform doctor if you are suffering from any severe medical condition. You should never use this medicine to treat an asthma attack that has already begun as it may cause life threatening condition. Tremor, insomnia (difficulty in sleeping) and dry mouth are some side effects of this medicine.
