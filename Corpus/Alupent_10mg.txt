Use this medicine regularly to get the most benefit from it even if you feel well.Alupent 10mg Tablet is generally safe and effective but some common side effects include headache. fast heart rate, tremor, and insomnia. These side effects are not often dangerous and they should gradually improve as your body gets used to this medicine.
